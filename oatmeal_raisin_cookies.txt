Ingredients
    4/3 cups gluten free oat flour
    1/2 tsp baking soda
    1/4 tsp salt
    1/2 tsp cinnamon
    1/4 cup sugar
    1/3 cup turbinado sugar
    1 tbsp flaxseed
    1/3 cup olive oil
    1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    1/2 cup gluten free rolled oats
    3/4 cup raisins

Instructions
    Blend flaxseed, then add 1/4 cup water

    Add to mixing bowl
        flour
        baking soda
        salt
        cinnamon
        sugar
        turbinado sugar
    Combine
    Add
        flaxseed mixture
        olive oil
        vanilla extract
    Beat until incorporated
    Add oats and raisins and beat until distributed
    Form into one large ball, then refrigerate for 30 minutes

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees
    Form dough into balls and place on baking sheet on baking tray
        Flatten into 1/2 inch high cylinders
    Bake for 12 minutes (until golden on the edges)
    Cool for 15 minutes
