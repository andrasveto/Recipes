Ingredients
    1 cup quinoa (yields 3 cups cooked)
    2 cups water

Instructions
    Rinse quinoa

    Add quinoa and water to saucepan
    Bring to boil covered (10 heat)
    Uncover
    Reduce heat and simmer until water absorbed (15 minutes) (3 heat)
    Remove from heat, cover, and let sit for 5 minutes
