Ingredients
    2 cup white, oat, or spelt flour
    1 tsp baking soda
    1/2 tsp salt
    1/3 cup sugar
    1/3 cup turbinado sugar
    1/3 cup chocolate chips
    1/4 cup oat/cashew/soy milk
    1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
    1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract

Instructions
    Add to bowl
        flour
        baking soda
        salt
        sugar
        turbinado sugar
    Combine
    Add
        milk
        olive oil
        vanilla extract
    Beat until incorporated
    Fold in chocolate chips
    Form into one large ball, then refrigerate for 30 minutes

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees
    Form dough into balls and place on baking sheet on baking tray
        Flatten into 1/2 inch high cylinders
    Bake for 12 minutes (until golden on the edges)
    Cool for 15 minutes
