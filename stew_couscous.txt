Ingredients
    4 onions (1kg chopped)
    8 cloves garlic (8 tsp chopped)
    1/2 cup olive oil
    8 tsp bouillon
    8 tbsp paprika
    4 cups brown/green lentils
    2 kg frozen mixed veggies (peas, carrots, green beans, corn)
    500g diced tomatoes
    2 tsp dried thyme
    8 bay leaves
    2 cup couscous

Instructions
    Rinse and drain lentils

    Heat olive oil in large pot over medium high heat (9 heat)
    Add onions
    Saute onions until lightly browned (20 minutes) (9 heat)
    Stir in garlic
    Saute for 1 minute (9 heat)

    Add 2 cups water
    Add lentils
    Add 18 cups water, paprika, bouillon, thyme, bay leaves, 10 tsp salt, 40 turns pepper
    Bring to boil (9 heat)
    Cook covered for 10 minutes (3 heat)
        Stir after 5 minutes

    Add frozen veggies, diced tomatoes
    Bring to boil (9 heat)
    Cook covered for 10 minutes (3 heat)
        Stir every 5 minutes

    Add couscous
    Bring to boil (9 heat)
    Cook covered for 5 minutes (3 heat)

    Turn heat to 6 and cook uncovered, stirring regularly, until reduced
    Turn off heat and let sit covered for a few hours
