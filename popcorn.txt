Ingredients
    1/3 cup corn kernels
    1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions
    Place olive oil and 5 kernels into large pot over medium heat
    Once all kernels pop, remove from heat and add remaining kernels
    Shake to distribute and wait 1 minute
    Return to heat, shaking regularly
    Remove from heat once several seconds pass between pops

    Transfer to bowl
    Drizzle with 2 tbsp olive oil
    Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp fine salt
    Toss
