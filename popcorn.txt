Ingredients
    1/4 cup corn kernels
    2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions
    Place olive oil and two kernels into large pot over medium heat
    Once both kernels pop, remove from heat and add remaining kernels
    Shake to distribute and wait 1 minute
    Return to heat, shaking regularly
    Remove from heat once several seconds pass between pops

    Transfer to bowl
    Drizzle with olive oil
    Sprinkle with 1/4 tsp fine salt
    Toss
