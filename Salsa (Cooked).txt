Ingredients
    2 medium tomatoes
    0.25 tsp habanero flakes
    5 cloves garlic
    2 tsp extra virgin olive oil

Instructions
    Core tomatoes, cut in half
    Mince garlic

    Put tomatoes, habanero, and garlic into food processor
    Add 1/4 cup water and 1 tsp salt
    Pulse until completely liquified

    Heat olive oil in sauce pan on medium high (6 heat)
    Add puree and bring to low simmer (2.5 heat)
    Cook uncovered until dark red, stirring occasionally (15 minutes)
