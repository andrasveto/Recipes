Ingredients
    30 oz canned diced fire-roasted tomatoes
    1 serrano or jalapeno pepper
    1 cup fresh cilantro
    1 lime
    1/2 white onion
    1 tsp ground cumin
    1 clove garlic

Instructions
    Slightly drain tomatoes
    Seed and chop pepper
    Chop cilantro
    Chop onion
    Mince garlic

    Add to food processor
        Tomatoes
        Pepper
        Cilantro
        Juice from lime
        Onion
        Cumin
        1/2 tsp salt
        Garlic
    Pulse to mix until desired texture
