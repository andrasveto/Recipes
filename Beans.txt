Ingredients
    1 lb dried black beans

Instructions
    Sort and rinse beans
    Place beans in large pot
    Cover with water 2 in above top of beans
    Cover pot, bring to boil (10 heat), then remove from heat
    Let rest for 1 hour

    Stir in 1.5 tsp kosher salt
    Bring to boil over medium heat (7 heat)
    Uncover, reduce heat, and simmer (4.5 heat)
        Add more water as necessary to keep beans submerged
        Done once beans are tender and creamy (1-1.5 hours)
