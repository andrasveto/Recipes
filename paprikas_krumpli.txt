Ingredients
    6 lbs red potatoes
    2 large onions
    4 cloves garlic
    5 tbsp paprika
    1/4 cup olive oil
    2 lbs tomatoes
    3 hungarian red pepper or bell pepper

Instructions
    Chop onions
    In a large pot heat the oil on medium high heat (8 heat)
    Add onions, saute until golden brown (20 minutes)

    Chop pepper
    Chop tomatoes
    Mince garlic
    Peel and wash potatoes, then cut into .5 inch squares
    Add garlic, saute (1 minute)
    Stir in paprika, then add tomatoes and pepper and stir
    Cook, stirring frequently (10 minutes) (6 heat)
    Add potatoes and stir (5 minutes)
    Add 8 cups water (or until potatoes covered by water)
    Add 4 tsp salt, 20 turns pepper
    Bring to boil covered (10 heat)
    Cook covered (50 minutes) (3 heat)
    Boil off excess liquid uncovered until desired consistency (5 heat)
    Turn off heat, stir, and let sit uncovered (10 minutes)
        Stir occasionally
