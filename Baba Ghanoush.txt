Ingredients
    1 medium eggplant
    1 large clove garlic
    1 lemon
    2 tbsp tahini

Instructions
    Mince garlic
    Juice lemon

    Preheat oven to high broil and position rack at top of oven

    Slice eggplant into 1/4 inch thick rounds
    Pat dry with paper towels
    Arrange on baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil and pinch salt
    Roast 4 minutes, turn, roast 4 minutes or until golden brown
    Remove from pan
    Stack rounds in bowl and cover (5 minutes)
    Peel away most of the skin (throw away)

    Add eggplant flesh to food processor
    Add juice from lemon, garlic, tahini, pinch salt
    Process until creamy, scraping sides and bottom of bowl intermittently
