Ingredients
    3 lbs yukon gold potatoes
    3 tbsp vegan butter

Instructions
    Wash and peel potatoes
    Slice potatoes in half. Place in large pot and fill with water until covered
    Add 1 tsp salt, bring to low boil covered (10 heat)
    Cook covered (20 minutes) (2 heat)
    Drain, then put in food processor
    Add vegan butter, 1 tsp salt, and 10 grinds pepper
    Pulse until smooth
