Ingredients
    1 lb yellow potatoes
    2 tsp vegan butter

Instructions
    Wash and peel potatoes
    Slice potatoes into small pieces.
    Place in large pot and fill with water until covered
    Bring to low boil covered (10 heat)
    Cook covered (20 minutes) (2 heat)
    Drain, then put in food processor
    Add butter, .5 tsp salt, 5 turns pepper
    Pulse until smooth (no more than 15 seconds)
