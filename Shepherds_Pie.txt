Ingredients
    1 medium onion
    2 cloves garlic
    1.5 cups brown/green lentils
    4 cups vegetable stock
    1 tsp dried thyme (or 2 tsp fresh)
    10 oz frozen mixed veggies (peas, carrots, green beans, corn)
    2 lbs potatoes for mashed potatoes (or cauliflower)

Instructions
    Dice onion
    Mince garlic
    Rinse and drain lentils
    Make stock with 5 tsp vegebase and 4 cups water

    Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in large pot over medium high heat (7 heat)
    Saute onions until lightly browned (5 minutes) (7 heat)
        Add a pinch salt and pepper
    Add garlic and saute (1 minute)
    Add lentils, stock, thyme, and stir
    Bring to low boil (10 heat)
    Reduce heat to simmer and cook uncovered for 30 minutes (2 heat)
    Preheat oven to 425
    Add frozen veggies, stir, and cook covered for 10 minutes (2 heat)
    Boil off excess liquid until viscosity of thick stew (5 heat)

    Start mashed potatoes after lentils start simmering

    Lightly grease 2-quart baking dish
    Transfer stew to prepared baking dish and top with mashed potatoes
    Smooth down with spatula and season with salt and pepper
    Place on baking sheet to catch overflow
    Bake until potatoes lightly browned on top (20 minutes)
    Let cool to thicken before serving
