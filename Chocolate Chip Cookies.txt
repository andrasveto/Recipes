Ingredients
    1 cup white, oat, spelt, or almond flour
    1/2 tsp baking soda
    1/4 tsp salt
    1/2 cup brown sugar (or 1/4 regular and 1/4 brown)
    1/4 cup chocolate chips
    2 tbsp oat/cashew/soy milk
    2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    1/4 tsp pure vanilla extract

Instructions
    Combine flour, baking soda, salt, sugar, and chocolate chips in bowl
    Stir in milk, olive oil, and vanilla extract
    Keep stirring until cookie-dough texture achieved
    Form into one large ball, then refrigerate for 2 hours

    Preheat oven to 375 F
    Form dough balls and place on baking sheet on baking tray
        Leave room between cookies so they can spread
    Bake 12 minutes on the center rack
    Let cool on baking tray 10 minutes
