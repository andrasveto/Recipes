Ingredients
    1 kg chopped onions
    8 tsp chopped garlic
    1/2 cup olive oil
    2 cups dry brown lentils
    1 kg frozen mixed veggies (peas, carrots, green beans, corn)
    500 g diced tomatoes
    2 cups cooked chickpeas
    4 tbsp paprika
    4 tsp bouillon
    1 tsp dried thyme
    4 bay leaves

Instructions
    Rinse and drain lentils

    Heat olive oil in large pot over medium high heat (6 heat)
    Add onions
    Saute onions until lightly browned (20 minutes) (6 heat)
    Stir in garlic
    Saute for 2 minutes (6 heat)

    Add lentils, veggies, tomatoes, chickpeas
    Add 6 cups water, paprika, bouillon, thyme, bay leaves, 5 tsp salt, 20 turns pepper
    Bring to boil covered (9 heat)
    Cook covered for 20 minutes (3 heat)
        Stir every 5 minutes

    Turn heat to 6 and cook uncovered, stirring regularly, until reduced
    Turn off heat and let sit covered for a few hours
