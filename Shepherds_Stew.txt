Ingredients
    2 onions
    2 cloves garlic
    4 tsp bouillon
    2 tbsp paprika
    2 lbs potatoes
    2 cups brown/green lentils
    1 lb frozen mixed veggies (peas, carrots, green beans, corn)
    1 tsp dried thyme (or 2 tsp fresh)
    4 bay leaves

Instructions
    Rinse and drain lentils
    Dice onion
    Mince garlic
    Peel potatoes and chop into .5 inch cubes

    Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in large pot over medium high heat (7 heat)
    Saute onions until lightly browned (10 minutes) (7 heat)
    Stir in garlic and paprika
    
    Add 6 cups water, bouillon, potatoes
    Bring to boil covered (10 heat)
    Cook covered for 10 minutes (2 heat)

    Add lentils, thyme, bay leaves, 2 tsp salt, 20 turns pepper
    Bring to boil (10 heat)
    Cook covered for 10 minutes (2 heat)

    Add frozen veggies
    Bring to boil (10 heat)
    Cook covered for 20 minutes (2 heat)
    Turn off heat and let sit for 10 minutes
