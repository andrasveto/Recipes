Ingredients
    1 3/4 cups gluten free oat flour
    6 tbsp brown sugar
    1/2 tsp salt
    1/2 tsp baking soda
    4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    4 tbsp water (UNTESTED possibly 3)
    jam for serving (raspberry preferred)

Instructions
    Preheat oven to 350 F

    Combine
        oat flour
        brown sugar
        salt
        baking soda

    Stir in
        olive oil
        water

    Form dough into balls and place on baking sheet on baking tray
        flatten, then make thumbprint in center to hold jam

    Bake for 14 minutes at 350 F
    Remove and let cool
    Once cool, serve with jam
