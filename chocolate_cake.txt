Ingredients
    2 cups oat flour
    0.75 cup cocoa powder
    1 tsp baking soda
    1 tsp salt
    1 cup sugar
    1 medium apple (or 0.5 cup apple sauce)
    0.5 cup extra virgin olive oil
    1.5 tsp vanilla extract
    0.5 cup jam (raspberry preferred)

Instructions
    Peel and chop apple.
    Add to saucepan with 1/2 cup water and cook on 3 heat for 30 minutes
    Add to blender and blend until smooth

    Preheat oven to 350 F

    Grease two 8 inch round pans
    Cut round baking paper onto the bottom of the pans

    In large bowl, combine
        flour
        cocoa powder
        baking soda
        salt
        sugar
    In separate bowl, whisk
        oil
        applesauce
        1.5 cups water
        vanilla extract
    Pour wet into dry and stir until just combined
        Don't over-mix
    Pour into greased pans

    Bake on center rack for 30 minutes
    Let cool on rack
    Put in fridge overnight

    Use spatula to separate sides of pans from cake
    Invert each cake onto a large plate
    Remove paper
    Spread 3 tbsp jam between the two layers
    Spread 5 tbsp jam on top and sides
