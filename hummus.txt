Ingredients
    4 cups overcooked chickpeas
    3 lemons (1/3 cup lemon juice)
    1 cup stirred tahini
    2 cloves garlic
    2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    2 tsp ground cumin
    2 tsp salt (if eating with salted crackers. otherwise, add more)

Instructions
    Defrost chickpeas (if necessary)
    Mince garlic

    Use food processor at speed 2

    Add to food processor
        juice from lemon
        tahini
        olive oil
        cumin
        salt
        garlic
    Process for 1 minute (or more)
    Add chickpeas
    Add water and process until smooth (more water meakes it smoother)
        If refrigeration is desired, add water until almost liquid
