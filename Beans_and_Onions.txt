Ingredients
    1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    1 onion
    3 cups cooked black beans
    1 tsp cumin

Instructions
    Finely dice onion
    Rinse and drain beans

    Heat oil in large pan on medium-high heat (6 heat)
    Add onions and saute until beginning to brown (3 minutes)
    Add beans, cumin, 1 tsp salt, 0.5 cups water
    Bring to boil (10 heat), then simmer uncovered for 10 minutes (2.5 heat)
    Mash some of the beans to thicken mixture, but leave most intact
