Ingredients
    4 cups dry garbanzo beans (makes 12 cups cooked)
    0.5 tsp baking soda

Instructions
    Rinse garbanzo beans and soak overnight in water
    Drain and rinse

    Bring 16 cups water to rolling boil (10 heat)
    Add hydrated beans and baking soda
    Boil covered until tender (1 hour) (3 heat)
