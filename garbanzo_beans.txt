Ingredients
    2 cups dry garbanzo beans (makes 6 cups cooked)
    1 tsp baking soda

Instructions
    Rinse garbanzo beans and soak overnight in 4 cups water
    Drain and rinse

    Bring 4 cups water to rolling boil (10 heat)
    Add hydrated beans and baking soda
    Boil covered until tender (3 hours) (3 heat)
