Ingredients
    4 cup lentils
    4 cup wild rice
    4 large onions
    2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil

Instructions
    Dice onions
    In large pot, heat olive oil (6 heat)
    Add onions and cook uncovered until dark golden brown/black (40 minutes)

    Wash and soak lentils and rice

    Pour 12 cups water over onions
    Stir in rice and lentils
    Stir in 8 tsp salt and 40 turns pepper
    Cover and bring to boil (6 heat)
    Reduce heat and simmer covered (2 heat)
    Done once liquid absorbed and rice cooked (50 minutes)
    To finish rice, turn off heat (still covered) and let sit (10 minutes)
