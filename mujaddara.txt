Ingredients
    4 cup lentils
    4 cup basmati rice
    4 large onions (about 1kg diced)
    1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Instructions
    Wash and soak lentils and rice
    Dice onions
    
    In large pot, heat olive oil (9 heat)
    Add onions and cook uncovered until dark golden brown/black (40 minutes)

    Pour 11 cups water over onions
    Stir in rice and lentils
    Stir in 8 tsp salt and 40 turns pepper
    Cover and bring to boil (9 heat) (25 minutes)
    Reduce heat and simmer covered (2.5 heat)
    Done once liquid absorbed and rice cooked (20 minutes)
    Once done, immediately put in baking trays or small containers to cool off
