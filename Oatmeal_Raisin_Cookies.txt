Ingredients
    1 cup white, oat, or spelt flour
    1/2 tsp baking soda
    1/4 tsp salt
    1/2 tsp cinnamon
    1/4 cup sugar
    1/2 cup turbinado sugar
    1 tbsp flaxseed
    1/3 cup olive oil
    1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    3/2 cups old fashioned rolled oats
    3/4 cup raisins

Instructions
    Add to food processor
        flour
        baking soda
        salt
        cinnamon
        sugar
        brown sugar
        flaxseed
    Process for 3 minutes

    Transfer to mixing bowl
    Add
        3 tbsp water
        olive oil
        vanilla extract
    Beat until incorporated
    Add oats and raisins and beat until distributed
    Form into one large ball, then refrigerate for 2 hours

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees
    Portion cookie dough onto parchment
    Bake for 12 minutes (until golden on the edges)
    Cool for 15 minutes
