Ingredients
    28 oz diced tomatoes (canned)
    3 cloves garlic
    1 tbsp olive oil
    .5 tsp oregano
    .25 tsp thyme
    10 turns pepper
    1 cup vegetable broth (made with 1 tsp base)

Instructions
    Mince garlic

    Add everything to medium saucepan and bring to simmer covered (5 heat)
    Simmer uncovered for 1 hour, stirring occasionally (3.25 heat)
