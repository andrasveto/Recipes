Ingredients
    4 medium avocados
    1/2 white onion
    1/4 cup fresh cilantro
    1 small jalapeno
    1.5 limes
    1/4 tsp ground coriander

Instructions
    Halve and pit avocados
    Chop onion
    Chop cilantro
    Seed and chop jalapeno

    Scoop flesh of avocados into serving bowl
    Use fork to mash avocade until desired texture
    Add onion, cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice, coriandor, and 1 tsp salt
    Stir to combine
