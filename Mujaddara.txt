Ingredients
    1 cup lentils
    1 cup long grain rice
    2 large onions
    2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

Instructions
    Dice onions
    In large pot, heat olive oil (7 heat)
    Add onions and cook until dark golden brown (30 minutes)
        Sprinkle with 1 tsp salt and stir often

    While onions cook
        Soak rice for 15 minutes, then drain

        Sort and rinse lentils
        Place in small pan with 2 cups water
        Bring to boil (10 heat), then simmer (4.5 heat)
        Cover until par-boiled (10 minutes)
        Drain

    Pour 2 cups water over onions, bringing to boil (10 heat)
    Reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes (4.5 heat)
    Stir in rice and lentils
    Cover and bring to boil (10 heat)
    Stir in salt and pepper
    Reduce heat and simmer (4.5 heat)
    Done once liquid absorbed and rice cooked (20 minutes)
