Ingredients
    1 lbs potatoes
    .75 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    1 tsp smoked paprika
    1 clove garlic
    1 tsp salt
    10 turns pepper

Instructions
    Preheat oven to 450 F
    Mince garlic
    Peel potatoes. Slice into 1-1.5 in wedges
    Place into gallon-sized plastic zipper bag with olive oil
    Toss until lightly coated with oil
    Add spices and garlic
    Toss until evenly coated

    Lightly brush baking sheet with olive oil
    Spread potatoes out in an even layer, flat surfaces touching sheet
    Roast in oven until cooked through (25 minutes)
