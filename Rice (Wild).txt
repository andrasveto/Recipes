Ingredients
    .5 cup dry wild rice (Lundberg wild blend)

Instructions
    Combine rice with 1 cup water in pot and bring to boil covered (10 heat)
    Reduce heat to low simmer, cook 45 minutes covered (1 heat)
    Remove from heat (still covered) and let sit for 10 minutes.
    Fluff with fork

Ratios
    Rice    .5    1       2
    Water   1     1.75    3.5
