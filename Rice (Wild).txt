Ingredients
    .5 cup dry wild rice (Lundberg wild blend)

Instructions
    Combine rice with 1 cup water in pot with lid and bring to boil (10 heat)
    Reduce heat to low simmer, cook 45 minutes (1 heat)
    Remove from heat (with lid on) and let sit for 10 minutes.
    Fluff with fork

Ratios
    Rice    .5    1       2
    Water   1     1.75    3.5
