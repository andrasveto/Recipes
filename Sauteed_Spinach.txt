Ingredients
    1 lb spinach
    2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    3 cloves garlic

Instructions
    Clean spinach
    Thinly slice garlic

    Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium high heat (6 heat)
    Add garlic and saute until begins to brown (1 minute)
    Add spinach, turning to coat with oil
    Cover and cook (1 minute)
    Uncover and turn, then cover and cook (1 minute)
    Remove from heat, drain excess liquid
