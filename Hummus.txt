Ingredients
    2 cups cooked chickpeas
    1 lemon
    1/3 cup stirred tahini
    1 small clove garlic
    2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
    1 tsp ground cumin
    1 tsp salt

Instructions
    Mince garlic

    Use food processor at speed 1 unless otherwise noted

    In food processor, process tahini and juice from lemon (1 minute)
    Scrape sides and bottom of bowl, then process (30 seconds)
    Add olive oil, garlic, cumin, and salt, then process (30 seconds)
    Scrape sides and bottom of bowl, then process (30 seconds)
    Add 1 cup chickpeas, then process (1 minute)
    Scrape sides and bottom of bowl, add remaining chickpeas
    Process until thick and smooth (1-2 minutes)
    With food processor on, slowly add 2-3 tbsp water until desired consistency
    Process at 2 speed until smooth
